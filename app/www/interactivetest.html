Interactive Road Safety Quiz

Here is the learning tool to help make you a genius on vehicle safety. After taking this free 15-question quiz you will know what children need for vehicle safety and what safety precautions must be taken before entering a vehicle.

We offer multiple choice and true/false questions with hints to help out and explanations to answers filled with facts, legal information, and advice. And the best part: the quizzes are free! We understand the importance of continuous learning, particularly when it comes to being safe. By offering this quiz, we hope that people will enter a car with more knowledge than they previously had of it, and in turn prevent injuries and save lives.
